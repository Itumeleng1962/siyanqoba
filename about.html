About Us

At Ukwakhile, we're committed to shaping futures and empowering individuals and businesses through comprehensive training and consultancy services. With a focus on open training and learnership, we strive to equip our clients with the skills, knowledge, and support necessary to thrive in today's dynamic landscape.

What We Do

  • Open training and learnership programmes tailored to industry needs
  • Skills development, curriculum design, and course development
  • Business advisory and BBBEE consulting for sustainable growth
  • Blended and online learning solutions for flexible delivery
  • Ongoing learner and client support to ensure measurable outcomes

Who We Are?

Ukwakhile is a dynamic firm specializing in open training and learnership solutions. With a commitment to empowering individuals and organizations, we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the evolving needs of the modern business landscape. From skills development to business advisory and BBBEE consulting, we strive to be the partner of choice for those seeking excellence in training and consultancy.

We continuously improve the quality of our services by keeping abreast of latest trends and technologies. Our experienced and accredited personnel are highly knowledgeable in their fields.

Our Story

With combined experience spanning over 20 years, we are a training and business consultancy company accredited with QCTO on various qualifications. We have a BEE level 2 recognition. Our services cut across all sectors of the economy, from mining, manufacturing, transport, construction, pharmaceutical and retail; to name a few.

Our Mission

To increase productivity and profitability of South Africa, Africa and the world through custom made training and consulting solutions

Our Vision

To be the preferred training and consultancy company – recognized for adding value, improving performance and profitability.

Our Values

Integrity & Reliable

We will conduct ourselves with integrity, be transparent, reliable, and committed to our clients, staff and suppliers.

Client Orientated

Respect & Dignity

Accountability & Innovative
