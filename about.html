About Us

At Guardian Angel Studios, we're committed to shaping futures and empowering individuals and businesses through comprehensive training and consultancy services. With a focus on open training and learner ship, we strive to equip our clients with the skills, knowledge, and support necessary to thrive in today's dynamic landscape.

Who We Are?

Guardian Angel Studios is a dynamic firm specializing in open training and learnership solutions. With a commitment to empowering individuals and organizations, we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the evolving needs of the modern business landscape. From skills development to business advisory and BBBEE consulting, we strive to be the partner of choice for those seeking excellence in training and consultancy.

We continuously improve the quality of our services by keeping abreast of latest trends and technologies. Our experienced and accredited personnel are highly knowledgeable in their fields.

Our Story

With combined experience spanning over 20 years, we are a training and business consultancy company accredited with QCTO on various qualifications. We have a BEE level 2 recognition. Our services cut across all sectors of the economy, from mining, manufacturing, transport, construction, pharmaceutical and retail; to name a few.

We operate in all 9 South African provinces and Africa through our mobile offices and partners, catering for both private and public organizations. We attract clientele from the Metropolitan municipalities, corporate world as well as from rural areas.
