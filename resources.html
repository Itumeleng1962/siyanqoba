Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and services

What is Siyanqoba Training & Development?

Siyanqoba is an occupational training and development company that provides comprehensive training services across various industries. We offer accredited qualifications, skills programmes, and short courses to help individuals and organizations achieve their professional development goals.

Are your courses accredited?

Yes, our courses are SETA accredited and registered on the National Qualifications Framework (NQF). This ensures that your certification is nationally recognized and will enhance your career prospects.

Do you offer online training?

Absolutely! We offer flexible learning options including virtual classrooms, blended learning, and fully online courses. Our virtual training sessions are conducted live with expert facilitators via platforms like Zoom and MS Teams.

How long do courses typically take to complete?

Course duration varies depending on the qualification level and type. Short courses can be completed in 1-5 days, skills programmes typically take 2-6 weeks, and full qualifications may take 3-12 months including workplace experience.

What materials are included with my course?

All courses include comprehensive study materials, workbooks, and relevant software licenses where applicable. For practical courses, we also provide access to necessary equipment and tools during training sessions.

Can I switch between delivery methods?

Yes, we offer flexible delivery methods. Depending on course availability and your circumstances, you may be able to switch between in-classroom, virtual, or blended learning options. Please speak with our consultants for specific arrangements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards, company purchase orders, and installment plans. For corporate clients, we can arrange customized payment terms.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for individual students. You can split your course fees into manageable monthly installments. Contact our finance team to discuss options that work for your budget.

Are there any additional costs?

Our course fees are comprehensive and include study materials, assessments, and certification. Some specialized courses may have additional costs for external examinations or professional body registrations, which will be clearly communicated upfront.

How will I receive my certificate?

Upon successful completion of your course and assessments, your certificate will be posted to your registered address. You'll also receive a digital copy via email. Processing typically takes 4-6 weeks after course completion.

Will my qualification be recognized by employers?

Yes, all our qualifications are registered on the National Qualifications Framework (NQF) and are recognized by employers across South Africa. Your achievements are also recorded on the National Learner Database.

Can I get a replacement certificate?

Yes, replacement certificates can be issued for a small administrative fee. You'll need to provide proof of identity and complete a replacement request form. Processing takes 2-3 weeks.
